    For those of you who are new to Runyon’s Thanksgiving, we often have the most need for volunteers to drive a delivery route on Thanksgiving Day. If you volunteer to be a driver, will assign you a time in the mid-late morning to arrive at Runyon’s and pick up the meals. We will provide you with a route of generally 4-8 addresses in a designated area and ample directions and instructions for delivery to the clients. We prefer two adults to be present for all delivery routes.

    If you are interested in volunteering in-house on Thanksgiving morning, you will assist in packaging items at Runyon’s in the early morning for the meals to be delivered.
